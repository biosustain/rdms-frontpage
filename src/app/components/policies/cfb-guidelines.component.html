Introduction to Research Data Management at CfB

Research data management (RDM) is much more than simply storing your data in a public repository after publication. Research data management is about retaining and increasing the value of data as it is used, stored and reused appropriately; It is about the publication of a plethora of different and important scientific insights, through collaborations and knowledge sharing.

 Policy of the Retention of Primary Materials and Data at the CfB

Data and primary materials should be retained, stored and managed in a clear and accurate form that allows the result to be assessed, the procedures to be retraced adn -when relevant and applicable- the research to be reproduced. -quote from: The Danish Code of Conduct for Research Integrity, Data Management
