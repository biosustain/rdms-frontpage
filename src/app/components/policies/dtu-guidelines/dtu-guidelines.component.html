DTU's Guidelines and Policies

Introduction to Data Management

Research data management is good practice in order to ensure high quality in research. Data management supports that your research is carried out efficiently, and that data is handled securely throughout the entire research project. A data managementplan ensures that your research meets the requirements of DTU's policies, from funding agencies and from publishers.

At the DTU there is a lot of resources to be found that will give you general guidance on how to carry out research data management in compliance with DTU and other requirements. available to you though DTU inside.
